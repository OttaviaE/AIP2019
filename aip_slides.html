The D-score

D-score core procedure

\(D_{pratice} = \frac{M_{b6} - M_{b3}}{sd_{b6,b3}}\)

\(D_{test} = \frac{M_{b7} - M_{b4}}{sd_{b7,b4}}\)

\(D_{score} = \frac{D_{pratice} + D_{test]}}{2}\)
